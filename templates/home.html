Kelly McAllister

Voice Over Talent

Kelly is a professional actor and voice talent with experience in puppeteering. She is currently based in Fredericton, New Brunswick and is represented by Ta-Da Voiceworks. She attended the Ottawa Theatre School Conservatory before graduating from Sheridan College. Past credits include Rose Nylund in Thank You For Being a Friend (Toronto Centre for the Performing Arts), Cat/Vanity in The Blue Bird (Theatre Aquarius Studio), and Kate Monster in Avenue Q (Theatre Erindale).
